Note for Parents:

This goal-setting activity helps children develop financial discipline and delayed gratification - key concepts in Walter Clarke's 401Kid. By setting specific goals and creating a plan to achieve them, children learn that patience and persistence lead to rewards.

To enhance this activity:

  • Help your child set a realistic goal that's achievable within a reasonable timeframe
  • Create a visual savings tracker to accompany this worksheet (like a thermometer chart)
  • Discuss the difference between short-term and long-term goals
  • Consider matching a portion of their savings to encourage the habit
  • Celebrate milestones along the way to maintain motivation

As Clarke emphasizes in the book, teaching children to delay gratification builds the foundation for financial success. This worksheet helps children experience the satisfaction of working toward something meaningful rather than spending impulsively.
