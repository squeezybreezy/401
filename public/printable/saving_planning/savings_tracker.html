Note for Parents:

This savings tracker helps children visualize their progress toward a financial goal - a powerful motivator for developing the habit of saving. As Walter Clarke emphasizes in 401Kid, tracking progress is essential for building financial discipline.

To enhance this activity:

  • Help your child set up a dedicated savings container (jar, piggy bank, etc.) for this specific goal
  • Establish a regular "money check-in" time to update the tracker together
  • Celebrate milestone achievements with small rewards or recognition
  • Discuss how it feels to watch savings grow over time
  • Point out the connection between patience (delayed gratification) and reaching financial goals

This activity reinforces the book's emphasis on consistency and discipline as foundational traits for financial success. By tracking their savings, children experience firsthand how small, consistent actions lead to significant results over time.
