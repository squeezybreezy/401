Note for Parents:

This activity helps children practice counting money and making change - essential skills for financial literacy. As Walter Clarke emphasizes in 401Kid, understanding the basic mechanics of money is the foundation for more complex financial concepts later.

Extend this activity by:

  • Using real coins to practice counting
  • Setting up a pretend store at home where your child can practice making purchases and calculating change
  • Discussing how saving even small amounts of change can add up over time
  • Introducing the concept of a piggy bank or savings jar for collecting coins

These hands-on activities reinforce the concept that money is a tool we can learn to use wisely, which aligns with the core message of financial discipline in the book.
