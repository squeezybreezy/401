Hello, Money Explorer!

Money is like a special tool that helps us get things we need and want. Before we can use money, we need to know what different coins look like and how much they're worth. Let's learn about coins together!

Note for Parents:

This activity helps children recognize different coins and understand their values. After completing this worksheet, try these follow-up activities:

  • Have your child sort real coins by type
  • Practice counting small amounts with actual coins
  • Discuss what each coin can buy in today's world
  • Talk about how saving even small coins can add up over time

Remember, as Walter Clarke emphasizes in 401Kid, early money conversations create a foundation for lifelong financial literacy. Make these activities fun and engaging to foster a positive relationship with money concepts.
