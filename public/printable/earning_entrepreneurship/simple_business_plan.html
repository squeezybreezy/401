Note for Parents:

This simple business plan activity helps children apply entrepreneurial concepts from Walter Clarke's 401Kid in a practical way. By working through this plan, children learn to think systematically about creating value and earning income.

To enhance this activity:

  • Help your child identify a business idea that matches their interests and abilities
  • Discuss real-world examples of small businesses and how they operate
  • Consider providing a small "startup loan" that your child must repay from business earnings
  • Use this as an opportunity to discuss profit, loss, and the value of reinvestment
  • Connect the business planning process to the "think like an owner" concept from the book

As Clarke emphasizes, entrepreneurship teaches children valuable life skills beyond just making money—it develops creativity, problem-solving, resilience, and financial literacy in a hands-on way that children find engaging and meaningful.
