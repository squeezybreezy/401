How to Use This Calculator

This interactive tool helps you set and track financial goals based on concepts from Walter Clarke's 401Kid book. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter information about your financial goal (what you're saving for)
  2. See how long it will take to reach your goal with regular contributions
  3. Save your goals to track your progress over time
  4. Update your progress as you save more money
  5. Watch as you get closer to achieving your financial dreams!
