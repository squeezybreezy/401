How to Use This Challenge Center

This interactive tool helps your family practice financial concepts from Walter Clarke's 401Kid book through fun challenges. Here's how it works:

  1. Enter your family name to get started
  2. Browse challenges filtered by age group, category, or difficulty
  3. Complete challenges together as a family
  4. Mark challenges as complete to earn points
  5. Track your progress and add your own custom challenges
