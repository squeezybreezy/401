How to Use This Simulator

This interactive tool helps you practice entrepreneurship concepts from Walter Clarke's 401Kid book. Here's how it works:

  1. Choose a type of business to start
  2. Make decisions about inventory, pricing, and marketing
  3. Advance week by week to see how your business performs
  4. Respond to random events and market changes
  5. Try to grow your business and make a profit!
